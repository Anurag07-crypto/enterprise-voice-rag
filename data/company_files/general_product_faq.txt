Title: General Product FAQs

Q: What does NovaAI actually do?
A: It helps users build, deploy, and monitor ML models without managing infrastructure.

Q: Who is this product for?
A: Mainly data scientists, ML engineers, and AI startups.

Q: Is coding required?
A: Yes, basic Python knowledge is expected.

Q: Can non-technical users use it?
A: Not fully. Some UI features exist, but it's still technical.
