Document ID: HR-001
Title: Leave Policy
Company: TechNova AI

Employees are entitled to 20 paid leaves per calendar year.

Leave Types:
- Casual Leave: 10 days
- Sick Leave: 7 days
- Earned Leave: 3 days

Unused leaves cannot be carried forward.

Approval Process:
- Leaves longer than 2 days require manager approval.
- Emergency sick leave can be taken without prior notice.

Note:
Frequent absenteeism may impact performance reviews.
