Title: Model Deployment Guide
Product: NovaAI Platform

Steps to deploy a model:
1. Upload model file (.pkl or .onnx)
2. Configure environment settings
3. Assign model ID
4. Click "Deploy"

Deployment Time:
- Typically 2–5 minutes

Notes:
- Only models under 500MB are supported
- GPU deployment available for premium users
