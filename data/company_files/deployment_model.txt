Title: Model & Deployment FAQs

Q: What model formats are supported?
A: .pkl and .onnx

Q: Is there a size limit?
A: Yes, models must be under 500MB.

Q: How long does deployment take?
A: Usually between 2–5 minutes.

Q: Can I redeploy a model?
A: Yes, but it will overwrite the existing version.
