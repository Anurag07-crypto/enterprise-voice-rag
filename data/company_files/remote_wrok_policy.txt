Document ID: HR-002
Title: Remote Work Policy
Company: TechNova AI

Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week.

Rules:
- Mandatory office presence on Mondays and Fridays.
- Employees must be available between 10 AM - 5 PM IST.
- Work-from-home requests must be logged in the HR portal.

Exceptions:
- Fully remote roles may be granted based on project requirements.
