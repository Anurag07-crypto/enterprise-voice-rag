Title: Product Overview
Product: NovaAI Platform

NovaAI is an AI-powered platform that enables users to build, deploy, and manage machine learning models at scale.

Core Features:
- Model training and evaluation
- API-based deployment
- Real-time inference
- Monitoring and logging

Target Users:
- Data Scientists
- ML Engineers
- AI Startups

The platform supports Python-based workflows and integrates with major ML libraries.
