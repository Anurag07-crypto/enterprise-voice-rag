Title: Leave Policy (Revised)
Company: TechNova AI

Employees are entitled to 24 paid leaves annually.

Leave Breakdown:
- Casual Leave: 12 days
- Sick Leave: 8 days
- Earned Leave: 4 days

Carry Forward:
- Up to 5 unused leaves can be carried forward.

Approval:
- Manager approval required for leaves exceeding 3 days.
