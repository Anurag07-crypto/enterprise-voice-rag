Title: Mixed Context FAQs

Q: Can I deploy a model and use it without API?
A: No, predictions are only accessible via API.

Q: If I upgrade to Pro, will my old models get GPU?
A: Yes, but you need to redeploy them.

Q: Can I take leave and still access NovaAI?
A: Yes, product access is independent of HR policies.

Q: What happens if I exceed API limit?
A: Requests will fail until quota resets or you upgrade.
